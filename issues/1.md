# Ice rink and game shots trackers not centered at top of screen

## Bug Description

The ice rink is not centered along with the game shots trackers at the top of the screen.

## Current Behavior

- Ice rink and game shots trackers are not properly centered at the top of the screen
- Alignment issues occur in both landscape and portrait modes

## Expected Behavior

- The ice rink should be centered at the top of the screen
- Game shots trackers should be centered along with the ice rink
- Centering should be maintained regardless of whether the device is in landscape or portrait mode

- [ ] Ice rink is horizontally centered on the screen

- [ ] Centering works correctly in portrait mode
